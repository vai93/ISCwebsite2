About ISC

Powering Industry with Precision for 45+ Years

Indian Switchgears and Consultants (ISC) is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company based in Vadodara, Gujarat, delivering reliable, high-quality electrical control panels (LT) and automation panels since 1978.

Founded by a technocrat with deep field experience in India and abroad, ISC stands for engineering excellence, quality manufacturing, and long-term customer trust. At ISC, we believe an electrical panel is the core of any system—and its design demands both technical precision and creative insight.

Our recent recognition as a SIEPAN ELITE Technology Partner of Siemens Ltd. further strengthens our position as a forward-thinking, future-ready organization.
